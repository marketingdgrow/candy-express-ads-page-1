Questions South Indian Couples Ask Before They Book

Everything you need to know before choosing your wedding photographer pricing, style, timelines, and what to expect.

A professional wedding photographer in South India covers the full wedding pre-wedding rituals, bridal preparation, the main ceremony, reception, and post-wedding portraits. Most packages combine candid wedding photography, traditional portraits, outdoor sessions, and a cinematic wedding videography film depending on the coverage chosen.

Candid wedding photography captures real, unposed moments as they happen naturally without directing the subjects. Traditional photography involves staged, planned shots. Most South Indian couples today choose a blend of both styles to cover every layer of their wedding day emotionally and formally.

Wedding photography packages in South India typically start from ₹75,000 for single-day coverage. Couples choosing comprehensive photography and videography together usually invest between ₹1,00,000 and ₹2,50,000 depending on days covered, team size, and film deliverables. Custom quotes available for luxury and destination weddings.

Most experienced wedding photographers in South India are booked six to twelve months in advance particularly for Muhurtham dates between October and March. Booking early secures your date and gives the photography team time to fully understand your wedding vision and cultural requirements.

A pre-wedding photoshoot typically includes a location session with the couple, professional lighting, fully edited photographs, and sometimes a short film. The session is designed to feel natural and unhurried giving the couple time to settle comfortably in front of the camera well before the wedding day itself.

Wedding videography in South India typically takes three to six weeks to deliver after the wedding. A short highlight reel is usually ready earlier, while the full-length wedding film takes longer depending on the number of events filmed and the complexity of the final edit.
